About

The question of us being alone is an old one. Answers have been showing up worldwide for eons. It's only us, this latest version of the modern human who are still in the dark. Well, no more. A lot of this site is from one person's experiences in this realm, autoethnographic, making the written style both introspective and outwardly analytical. But that's just me, these pages are open for comment, interaction, and the sharing of your own experiences and ideas. Consider this site a vehicle to help bring our experiences to light by name or anonymously.

If you're a person deep inside this space, current of former military, or special government ops with experiences to share - please feel free. If communication needs to be secure, we can do that too.


The only caveat is that all be civil. Trolls and haters will be blocked. This is a space for open inclusion about a fascinating topic, not a place to be a jerk. I'm sure you understand. I hope that you enjoy the writing.
